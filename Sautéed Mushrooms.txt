INGREDIENTS
2 tsp		extra-virgin olive oil
2 tbsp		butter
3 cloves 	garlic, roughly chopped
1 tbsp		freshly chopped rosemary
10 oz		shiitake mushrooms, woody stems trimmed and sliced 1/3"-thick
To taste	Kosher salt
To tast		freshly ground black pepper
3 tbsp		water (or broth)

DIRECTIONS
1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil and butter. Add in garlic and rosemary and stir briefly until fragrant, 30 seconds.
2. Add in mushrooms and stir to coat evenly in butter. Season with salt and pepper, and continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until mushrooms are lightly golden, 6 to 8 minutes.
3. Deglaze pan with water (or broth) and stir until fully evaporated. 
Season with more salt to taste and top with more herbs before serving, if desired.
