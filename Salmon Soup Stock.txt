Salmon Soup Stock (Garden Time)

Ingridents
300 g		salmon bone and fins
1 pc		sliced lemongrass
15 g		garlic
20 g		onions
15 g		ginger slices
3 cups		water

Instructions
1. Fry salmon with small amount of oil for 3 minutes (high heat), make sure the fish meat is turn golden and not more
2. Add ginger slices, garlic, onions and lemongrass and fry for 1 min
3. Add 3 cups of boiling water (to cover the fish and boiling water can slimmer white soup)
4. Simmer for 30 - 40 min
5. Take out lemongrass, garlic and ginger
6. Filter out fish meat, optional
