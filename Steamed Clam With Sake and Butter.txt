Steamed Clam with Sake and Butter (MASA's Cooking ABC)

Ingredients
300 g		clams (11 oz)
3 pcs		garlic
1 pc		chili pepper
50 cc		sake (3 Tbsp)
1 Tbsp		butter
1 tsp		soy sauce
to taste	chopped green onion

Instructions
1. Fry garlic, chili pepper and white part of the green onion with oil
2. Add clam and sake, cook until the clams are open
3. Add soy sauce and butter
4. Serve with green onion
