Oyster Mushroom Cold Cut (Citron mother)

Ingredients
350 g		oyster mushroom


Insturctions

1. Slice mushroom into thin pieces.
2. Branch mushroom in hot water for 2-3 minutes
3. Dry out all the water.
4. Mince a small glove of garlic.
5. Mince a small piece of scallion.
6. Mince a small piece of cilantro.
7. Mince a small piece of red chili pepper without seed.
8. Mix with 2 Tbsp thick soy sauce, 1 tsp sugar and 1 Tbsp sesame oil.
9. Mix the sauce until the sugar disolve and top and mushroom.
