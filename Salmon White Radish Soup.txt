Salmon White Radish Soup (Graden time homemade cuisine)

Ingredients

300g		salmon
300g		white radish
600g		boiling water
3 Tbsp	oil

Seasonings
1/3 tsp	salt
1/2 tsp	fish sauce
1 tsp		white vinegar
some		chopped green onion

Instructions
1. Heat 600g of boiling water
2. Remove skin and cut radish into thin long pieces
3. Cut salmon to 2 pieces
4. Add oil to wok and fry salmon skin side down for 2 minutes
5. Turn over salmon and fry for 1 minute
6. Add boling water to wok
7. Add white radish and cook for 2 minutes
8. Add all seasonings
9. Serve and enjoy
