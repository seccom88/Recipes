Air Fryer Crispy Broccoli (bestrecipebox.com)

The broccoli will cook down and shrink, so if it the basket feels full at first, there will be half the amount after it shrinks. 

Servings: 4

Ingredients

1 pound (454 g) broccoli , cut into bite sized pieces
2 tablespoons (30 ml) oil (approximately)
1/2 teaspoon (2.5 ml) garlic powder
salt , to taste
Fresh cracked black pepper , to taste
Fresh lemon wedges

Instructions

1. Add broccoli to large bowl and drizzle evenly with olive oil. Season broccoli with garlic powder, salt and pepper.
2. Air Fry 380°F for 12-15 minutes, flipping and shaking 3 times through cooking. Or cook until crispy.  Serve with lemon wedges.
