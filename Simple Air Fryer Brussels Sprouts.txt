Simple Air Fryer Brussels Sprouts (allrecipes.com)

Ingredients
3/4 pound Brussels sprouts
1 tablespoon olive oil 
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

1. Preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C) for 5 minutes.

2. Place Brussels sprouts, olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl and mix well.

3. Spread evenly in the air fryer basket. Cook for 15 minutes, shaking the basket halfway through the cycle.
