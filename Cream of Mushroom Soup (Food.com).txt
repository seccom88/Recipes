CREAM OF MUSHROOM SOUP

INGREDIENTS
8 oz		fresh mushrooms
2 Tbsp	onions, chopped
1-2		garlic clove, minced
2 Tbsp	butter
2-3 Tbsp	flour (separated)
2 cups 	chicken broth
1 cup 	light cream or evaporated milk
1⁄2 tsp	salt
1⁄4 tsp	pepper
1⁄4 tsp	nutmeg

DIRECTIONS
1. Cut the mushrooms into slices.
2. Melt butter in large frying pan. Add in onions, garlic, and mushrooms. Cook until onions are soft.
3. Blend in 2 Tbsp flour and stir.
4. Add in the chicken broth and heat until slightly thickened while stirring frequently.
5. Stir cream with additional 1 Tbsp flour and seasonings. Add in cream to soup. Heat to thicken while stirring frequently.
6. Serve and enjoy!
