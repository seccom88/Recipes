Oyster with ginger and green orion

Ingredients
1 can		oyster
1 tsp		salt
1 Tbsp		oyster sauce
1/2 tsp		sugar
1/8 tsp		white pepper powder
1 tsp		starch
3 Tbsp		water

Instuction
1. Branch oyster with hot water for 2-3 minutes to 60 percent
2. coated oyster with starch and pan fry to golden
3. Fry ginger, garlic and green orion under medium heat
4. Add oyster
5. Add green part of the green orion
6. Add sauce
