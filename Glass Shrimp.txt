Glass shrimp with asparagus (TVB)

Ingredients
400 g		shrimps
1 tsp		salt
1 tsp		starch
1/2 		egg white
250 g		asparagus (cut into small pieces)
1 small dish	shrimp paste

Seasoning
1/4 to 1/2 tsp	salt
1/4 to 1/2 tsp	sugar
100 ml		chicken stock

Instructions

1. Clean the shrimps and cut from the top to remove the intestines.
2. Add water, 1 tsp of salt and 1 Tbsp starch the shrimps to change the shrimp to more solid texture. Store in refrigerator for 1 hour.
3. After 1 hour, wash the shrimps with enough until the shrimp turn translucent (hence glass).
4. Dry the shrimp with towel and leave the shrimps in the towel in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
5. Mix the shrimp with half egg white and store in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
6. Fry asparagus with small amount of oil
7. Add salt and sugar to taste
8. Add chicken stock to fully cook the asparagus.
9. Season the shrimp with 1/4 to 1/2 tsp of salt and sugar.
10. Heat oil in wok to 80% of max temperature.
11. Fry the shrimp in oil for 30 seconds until the shrimp turn red.
12. Add shrimp on top of the asparagus in plate and serve with shrimp paste.
