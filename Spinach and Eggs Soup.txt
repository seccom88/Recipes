Spinach and Eggs Soup (Chef Mao)

Ingredients
200g		spinach
2 pcs		eggs
1g		salt
2g		chicken essense
5g		starch
2g		sesame oil
10g		green orion
10g		ginger


Instructions
1. Clean and cut spinach into section

2. Beat eggs

3. Stir fry ginger and orion first, then add spinach

4. Add hot water to cover the spinach

5. Add salt (1 tsp), chicken essense (1 Tbsp)

6. Add starch with water slowly

7. Add beaten eggs slowly (eggs must in after the starch water)

8. The dish is ready to serve! Enjoy!
