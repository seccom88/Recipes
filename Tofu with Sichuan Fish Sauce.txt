Tofu with Sichuan fish sauce

Ingredients

1 Tbsp		Hot Sichuan bean paste
1 Tbsp		soy sauce
1 Tbsp		sugar

1 Tbsp		rice wine
1 Tbsp		black vingear
some		white pepper powder
4 Tbsp 		water

Instuctions

1. Mince garlic, ginger and scallion
2. Fry tofu 
3. Fry meat in wok with oil.
4. Add hot Sichuan bean paste, soy sauce, sugar and cook for 1 min.
5. Add rice wine, black vingear, white pepper powder and water.
