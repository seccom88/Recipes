Shogayaki (Precision Cooking)

Ingredients

300 g		poke belly with skin
20 g		ginger
40 g		sake
40 g		mirin
40 g		soy sauce
20 g		sugar
some 		cooking oil


Instruction

1. Make ginger into ginger sauce
2. Cut pork belly into thin strip
3. In a bowl, add ginger, sake, mirin, soy sauce and sugar, marinate the pork belly. Make sure the pork belly don't overlap too much
4. After 30 min, separate out the marinate and set aside
5. Fry the poke belly until done
6. Reduce the sauce in a pan
7. Add cooked pork belly and mix well with reduced sauce
8. Enjoy!
