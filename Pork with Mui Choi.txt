Pork with Mui Choi

Ingredients

300g 		mui choi
400g		pork belly
2 pcs		ginger
1 grove	garlic

Seasoning

1 tsp		shaohin rice wine
1 Tbsp		sweet soy sauce
50g		rock sugar
1 tsp		rice wine for branching

Instruction

1. Clean the mui choi by leave it in water for 10 minutes.
2. Cut the mui choi into small pieces and remember to remove the hard part close to the lower part of the mui choi root
3. Stir fry mui choi with small amount of oil and 1 pc of ginger until dry.
4. Add rock sugar to the dried mui choi
5. Branch the pork belly using cold water, 1 pc of ginger and rice wine, branch until the pork is white outside and red inside.
6. Stir fry the pork belly until brown. Add 1 pc of ginger and 1 pc of garlic, 1 tsp of shaohin wine and 1 Tbsp of sweet soy sauce
7. Add enough hot water to wok to cover the pork belly.
8. Add mui choi with rock sugar and bring the mixture to boil
9. Turn heat to low and slimmer for 1 hour
10. After an hour, turn heat to high to reduce the sauce and serve
