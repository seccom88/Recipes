Celtuce Stem Salad (Garden Time Homemade Cuisine)

Ingredients
350-400g	celtuce stem salad 

Sauce ingredients
1 tsp		minced garlic
1 tsp		sugar
1/4 tsp		salt
1/8 tsp		chicken essence
1/2 tsp		fish sauce
1/2 tsp		sesame oil
small amount	red pepper shred
1 tsp		lemon juice

Insturctions
1. Peel the skin of the celtuce stem and cut into long thin pieces
2. Cook in boiling water for 1 minute
3. Cool with cold water
4. Mix sauce ingredients in a separate bowl
5. Pour the sauce into the celtuce stem and keep in frig for 1/2 hour
6. Serve cold
