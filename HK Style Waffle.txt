Hong Kong Waffle (Eric's Bakery)

Ingredients
140g      cake flour
3g        baking powder 
80g       sugar
2 pcs     egg
50g       salted butter
160g      water
1g        vanilla extract

Instructions
1. Melt the butter using microwave or small pot

2. First mix all dry ingredients in a mixing bowl

3. Mix in liquid ingredients well

4. Rest the batter for 10 minutes to release the air bubble

5. Preheat the waffle maker for 5 minutes at max temperature

6. Bake the waffle maker to done (for 5 minutes)

7. Serve with peanut butter, sugar, condensed milk and addition butter
