Air Fryer Shrimp Paste Chicken Wings (Leisure Cooking)

Chicken Wings	6 pieces

Seasonings:
Shrimp Paste	1 tsp
Soy Sauce		1/2 tsp
Corn Starch	1 1/2 tsp
Sugar			1 tsp
Sesame Oil		small amount

1. Marinate the chicken wings with the seasonings for at least half an hour
2. Pre heat air fryer at 200C for 5 minutes
3. Place wing with skin on top into the air fryer.
4. Air fry at 200C for 20 minutes (for Recolte air fryer)
