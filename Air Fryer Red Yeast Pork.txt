Airfryer Red Yeast Pork (Ytower cooking channel)

Ingredients
1000g		pork belly
small amount	riander

Marinate
1/2 tsp		salt
1 tsp		sugar
1/8 tsp		white pepper powder
small amount	chinese five spices
45g (3 Tbsp)	red yeast sauce
1 Tbsp		garlic puree
1 Tbsp		rice wine

Instructions
1. Marinate pork for at least 3 hours or overnight
2. Clothed the pork with potate starch and let it sit for 5 minutes
3. Add some oil on the pork
4. Airfry at 180 C for 20 minutes
