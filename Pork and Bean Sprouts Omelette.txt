Pork and bean sprout omelette (Christine Ho)

Bean sprout	160 g
Salt 			small amount
Minced pork	90 g
Eggs			3 pieces
Water			2 Tbsp

Seasoning:
Soy sauce		1 tsp
Sugar			1/4 tsp
Corn starch	1/2 tsp
Oil			1 tsp

1. Cut out the head and tail of the bean sprout and wash. (Optional step)

2. Marinate the minced pork with the seasoing.

3. Fry the bean sprout until soft, add small amount of salt.

4. Fry the minced oil until done and set aside.

5. Beat the egg and mix in bean sprout and pork. Pan fry until done.
